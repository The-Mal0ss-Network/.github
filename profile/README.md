## About

The Mal0ss Network is a privately held multi-sector organization operating through a constellation of specialized subdivisions. The Network unifies interests across research, capital deployment, engineering, technology, and real estate — pursued with precision, and disclosed on our terms alone.

This GitHub presence is the public engineering surface of the Network. It hosts selected open work primarily from **Mal0ss Systems (CODEX)**, with contributions from adjacent divisions as they ship. Internal tooling, proprietary platforms, and client deliverables are not mirrored here.

## Principles

1. **Ship complete work.** Prototypes stay private. Public repositories represent finished or actively maintained software.
2. **Type everything.** Strict static typing is the default across languages that support it.
3. **Test what matters.** Unit, integration, and validation layers are not optional.
4. **Release properly.** Cross-platform binaries, signed where applicable, with clean changelogs.
